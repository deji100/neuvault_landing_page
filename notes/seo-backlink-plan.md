- Anchor guidance: `NeuVault`, `document vault app`

3. SaaSHub

## Suggested anchors

Use anchors that match the destination page intent.

Good examples:
- `NeuVault`
- `private document vault app`
- `scan and organize documents`
- `document reminder app`
- `find important documents faster`
- `secure document backup`

Avoid:
- repeated exact-match anchors on every backlink
- generic anchors like `click here`
- over-optimized anchors that do not fit the surrounding sentence

## Outreach sequence

1. Submit to Product Hunt and BetaList.
2. Cleanly list on SaaSHub and AlternativeTo.
3. Pitch 10 to 20 relevant roundup/review targets with page-specific angles.
4. Use /press in outreach to make asset and product info retrieval easy.
5. Ask for links to the page that best matches the article topic, not always the homepage.

## Junk to avoid

Do not spend time on:
- bulk directory submission packages
- obvious link farms
- irrelevant guest-post marketplaces
- low-quality app lists with no editorial standards
- spun listicles built only to sell backlinks
